Motion Typography
Busy Doing Nothing
Sep 2023
Printed matter, Motion graphic
I'm always busy doing nothing. This is a typographic animation project built around this paradoxical mantra. I created three distinct animations, each using a variety of typefaces and color palettes to express different moods. The strong visual contrast between the words “busy” and “doing nothing” highlights the tension and irony in the phrase—emphasizing how form can amplify meaning.
