This book is based on content from Wikipedia about Homo sapiens, reimagined through a cohesive visual system. I mainly used a green color palette, symbolizing the vitality and continuity of human life. I also used a mix of serif and sans-serif typefaces to create a clear typographic hierarchy—distinguishing titles and quotes from the body text—while reinforcing the balance between history and modernity in the narrative of our species.
