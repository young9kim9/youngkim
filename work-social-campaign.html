Imagine a World Without Hate
Digital, Motion graphic
I reimagined the Anti-Defamation League’s "Imagine a World Without Hate" campaign by translating its original video-based storytelling into a series of typographic posters across print, motion, and public space. The work uses juxtaposition and strikethrough typography to contrast lost lives with imagined futures, turning absence into a visible and reflective visual language.
